🎯 How to Bet on Ranked Wars

Welcome to The Colosseum! Here's how to participate in Torn City's first Ranked War betting market. Bet on faction outcomes, earn rewards, and become a legendary war predictor.

1

Connect Your Account

Link your Torn City account to verify your identity and enable betting. Your account must be at least 30 days old to participate.

2

Browse Active Wars

View current and upcoming ranked wars between factions. Each war has different betting options and odds based on faction strength.

3

Place Your Bets

Choose your prediction and stake your money. You can bet on faction wins, war duration, total damage, and other outcomes.

4

Watch & Collect

Monitor the war progress in real-time. If your prediction is correct, you'll receive your winnings plus any applicable bonuses.
